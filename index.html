About Me

I'm a student at Thompson Rivers University in Kamloops, BC. Over the summer, I worked as a full-stack web developer for a startup company in Kelowna. They have a web-based software which uses Bootstrap and Django to provide a way of tracking patient progress throughout mental health treatment.

Not only am I interested in web development, but I also enjoy teaching, tutoring, and mentoring. I have experience as a supplementary learning leader, math tutor, and volunteer support lab tutor. I recently started a volunteer position with Kamloops Immigrant Services as a computer mentor.

I have worked on various projects which can be found on my resume, as well as on my github. I enjoy learning and have attended hackathons and pursued personal projects such as www.wallgym.ca to improve my knowledge and skills.

I have strong skills in tools/technologies such as HTML, CSS/Sass, Javascript/Jquery, PHP, Bootstrap, and Python. I also have experience working with git, Jest, and Selenium.
